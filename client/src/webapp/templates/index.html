Body-fat & BMR calibration

Wave 2 (bulk/volume) calibration -- defaults reproduce today's numbers exactly; only override these if you know why.

TEF by macronutrients

Replaces the flat 10% TEF estimate with one computed from logged carb/fat/protein grams -- falls back to the flat estimate automatically for any week without macros logged.

Macro targets

Evidence-based per-kg-bodyweight targets: roughly 1.6-2.2 g/kg protein and 0.5-0.8 g/kg fat for a cut, 1.5-2.0 g/kg protein and 0.7-1.0 g/kg fat for a bulk. Carbs always fill the remainder of target calories -- there's no separate carbs setting.
